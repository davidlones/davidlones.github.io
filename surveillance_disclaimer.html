Renovation Documentary / Property Cleanup Media Consent Form

One form per identifiable person. This is a practical template for property cleanup, renovation, landscaping, exterior drone footage, before/after shots, and related documentary-style updates. It is not legal advice; get local legal review if the footage will be monetized, broadcast, or used in a formal real-estate campaign.

Drone / Exterior Footage

I understand that drone or exterior wide-angle footage may capture workers, vehicles, equipment, and general activity on the property. The project owner agrees to make reasonable efforts to respect the consent choice selected above before publishing or sharing footage.

Privacy and Safety

The project owner agrees not to intentionally record private conversations, personal phone calls, medical information, payment information, personal paperwork, license plates where avoidable, or private/off-duty activity unrelated to the work. No minors may be intentionally recorded without written consent from a parent or legal guardian.

Removal Request / Revocation

I may request that identifiable footage of me be removed, blurred, or cropped before publication. I understand that after footage has already been published, shared, downloaded, reposted, or archived, complete removal from all copies may not be possible.
