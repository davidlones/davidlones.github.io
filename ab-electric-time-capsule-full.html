A.B. Electric Time Capsule

Toni — I found traces of A.B. Electric online and it immediately brought me back.

I remember sitting at my Windows 98 computer, sending faxed images of space to your office. It felt like magic — something from my room crossing town through a phone line and appearing where you worked.

Seeing that office photo, the CRT, the paperwork, everything — it felt like stepping back into that exact moment in time.

This is just a small piece of that memory, preserved.

— David
