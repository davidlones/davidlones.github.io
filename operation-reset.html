Operation Birthday Reset

A four-week campaign to clear, sort, and polish the house before March 15. Not perfection. Not staging. Just breathable, open space.

Mission Framing

This is not a panic clean. It is a distributed reset. Each week has a focus. Each session is 2 hours. The house gets lighter gradually.

Weekly Arcs

WEEK 1 — Clear Surfaces & Floors

Remove visual noise. Clear counters. Sweep floors. Contain clutter into bins.

Goal: Visible space increases.

WEEK 2 — Sort & Reduce

Open boxes. Make decisions. Donate, trash, relocate. Reduce total object volume.

Goal: Fewer things in circulation.

WEEK 3 — Deep Clean & Organize

Wipe surfaces. Vacuum. Mop. Clean windows. Establish stable storage locations.

Goal: Clean base layer.

WEEK 4 — Final Polish

Baseboards, linens, final trash run, light decor reset. Make the house feel calm.

Goal: Birthday Ready.

2-Hour Session Template

Permanent Close Procedure (10 Minutes Nightly)

Sequence logic: Clear → Reduce → Clean → Polish. March 15 is the lighthouse. Everything before it is steady momentum.
